Our Core Services

Habilitation (HAB)

Skill‑building to increase independence: communication, daily living, social skills, community integration, and self‑advocacy. Sessions include measurable objectives and progress notes.

Attendant Care (ATC)

Personal care assistance such as bathing, grooming, dressing, toileting, meal prep, and mobility—delivered respectfully and safely in the least restrictive setting.

Respite

Short‑term coverage that gives primary caregivers time to rest, run errands, or attend to other responsibilities while knowing their loved one is supported.

Group Home Living

Safe, supportive residential environments with 24/7 supervision, individualized plans, community activities, and a focus on life skills and choice.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I qualify for services?

Services are authorized through Arizona’s Division of Developmental Disabilities (DDD). If you already have a DDD Support Coordinator, ask to include our agency in your plan. If not, we can guide you through eligibility and enrollment.

Do you accept ALTCS/Medicaid?

Many services are funded through ALTCS/Medicaid under the DDD program. We’ll verify funding and authorization before services begin.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the Phoenix West Valley and surrounding communities, including Avondale, Goodyear, and Buckeye. Ask about current openings.

How do you ensure quality and safety?

We follow HCBS rules, conduct background checks, require ongoing training, and complete routine supervisory visits and audits.
